The ansible-vault command allows file encription and requires a password to unencript.

$echo "this is abc" > secret.txt
$cat secret.txt
this is abc
$ansible-vault encript secret.txt
New Vault password:
Confirm New Vault password:
Encrytion seccessful

$cat secret.txt
sssssnnnnnndllllllllo999999elllddddddd999999dd99d
jkklkkkdfdfdfslfk;slkf;sdkf;sdkf;ksdfksd;fksdkf;d
kljfdsfjsfdskf;sdkf;skdf;ksd;fkds;fkd;skf;kf;dskf

$ansible-vault edit secret.txt
$ansible-vault view secret.txt
$ansible-vault decrypt secret.txt
