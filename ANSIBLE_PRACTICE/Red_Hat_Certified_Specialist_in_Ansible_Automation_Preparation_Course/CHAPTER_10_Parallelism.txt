It is possible to control the number of hosts acted upon at once time by Ansible
Ansible process will create forks to execute actions in parallel.
By default, the process will only for 5times. Default may be changed in ansible.cfg.

Number of forks canbe set for single command using -f.

===================
---
- hosts: labservers
  become: yes
  serial:
    - 1
    - 2
    - 50%
  tasks:
    - name: add hosts entry
      lineinfile:
        path: /etc/hosts
        line: "scoldham2 andible.mylabserver.com"

$ansible-playbook serial.yml

===================
 ---
- hosts: labservers
  become: yes
  serial:
    - 1
    - 2
    - 50%
  max_fail_percentage: 30   //allows you to cause a play to stop based on a certain(here 30%) failure threshold
  tasks:
    - name: add hosts entry
      lineinfile:
        path: /etc/hosts
        line: "scoldham2 andible.mylabserver.com"
